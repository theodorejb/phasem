Two-factor recovery codes

{{error}}

Recovery codes can be used to access your account in the event you lose access to your device and cannot receive two-factor authentication codes.

Treat your recovery codes with the same level of care as you would your password! We recommend saving them with a password manager such as Lastpass, 1Password, or Keeper.

Put these in a safe spot. If you lose your device and don't have the recovery codes you will lose access to your account.

  • {{code}}

When you generate new recovery codes, you must download or print the new codes. Your old codes won't work anymore.
